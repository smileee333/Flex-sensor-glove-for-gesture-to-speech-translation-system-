1/**
2 * @file sentences.h
3 * @brief All the declarations for data structures representing a sentence and methods associated with it are defined in this file.
4 *
5 * @details
6 * Sentence is represented as a collection of words. Here a sentence is represented as a double linked list such that each node of the list represents a word.
7 *
8 * A generic structure meant for representing a node in doubly linked list with following data-members:
9 * - data: a structure variable representing the data which represents a word.
10 * - next: a pointer to the next node in the list.
11 * - previous: a pointer to the previous node in the list.
12 *
13 * A generic structure meant for representing a word with following data-members:
14 * - a structure variable meant for storing the gesture id and space id. {gesture id, space id} together represent a unique word. It is used to play appropriate audio file for the word to be spoken.
15 * - a pointer pointing to a string representing the word in text format. It is used to display the word on the LCD display.
16 *
17 * A generic class template declaration meant for representing a sentence and the methods associated with it. Following data-members and member-functions are defined for this class template:
18 * - data-members: a pointer pointing to the head of sentence (doubly linked list), a pointer pointing to the tail of sentence (doubly linked list), a pointer pointing to any arbitirary word in the sentence
19 * an integer variable to keep track of number of words in the sentence.
20 *
21 * - member-functions: a. addWord(): To add word in the sentence doubly linked list.
22 * b. moveNext(): To move the ctrl pointer to next word in the sentence's double linked list.
23 * c. movePrevious(): To move the ctrl pointer to previous word in the sentence's double linked list.
24 * d. deleteWord(): To delete a word in the captured sentence.
25 * e. deleteSentence(): To delete the complete sentence.
26 * f. translateGesture(): To invoke the DF mini player module to play the audio files correponding to each captured word in sentence one by one.
27 *
28 * @author Smile Guleria
29 * @version 0.0.1
30 *
31 * @copyright Copyright (c) 2023 Smile Guleria
32 * @license MIT License
33 *
