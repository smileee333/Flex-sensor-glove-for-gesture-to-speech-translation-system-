3 * @brief All the declarations for data structures representing low power modes and methods associated with it are defined in this file.

5 * @details
6 *
7 * Typically two low power modes are definied here:
8 * - SLEEP_MODE_PWR_DOWN : meant for deep sleep during the times when no gesture capture is required as well as during the times when
9 * CPU needs to wait for single word translation to complete. CPU wakes-up from this deep sleep mode when start push button is pressed
10 * or DF mini player module's busy pin goes high.
11 * - SLEEP_MODE_IDLE : meant for sleep during the time interval between sampling process completion and wait for next sampling to start.
12 * CPU wakes from this sleep mode by simple TIMER interrupt
13 *
14 *
15 * An enum data type describing power modes supported by cpu
16 *
17 * A class meant for checking and configuring the power modes of cpu
18 * - data-members: mode: it represents the mode in which cpu is currently operating.
19 * - member-functions: a. To get the current power mode.
