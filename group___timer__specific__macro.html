Detailed Description

Macros for configuration of Timer control registers, oncompare register, Timer counting register, Timer interrupt mode register, Timer ISR.

To promote the idea of scalable architecture, Timer specific macro are defined here, to make following parameters to be configurable

  • a. Choice of Timer under consideration > It gives the tuning parameter for maximum possible Timer delay as per Timer under consideration. It also gives the tuning parameter for type of timer interruption to wake-up.
  • b. Prescalar value > Again it gives the tuning parameter under Timer under consideration, to scale the maximum possible Timer delay.
  • C. On compare regiser and value > it gives the another tuning paramerer under prescale value, to specifically choose a particular Timer delay within the range of (0 - max), such that max is specified by Timer under consideration and prescale value.
